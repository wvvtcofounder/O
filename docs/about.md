# About WVVT

WVVT is a low-power community radio station dedicated to amplifying local voices, independent music, and grassroots storytelling.

## Our Mission

To provide a free, accessible platform for community expression through the power of radio. We believe every neighborhood deserves a voice on the dial.

## How It Works

WVVT operates as a volunteer-driven station. Our programming is shaped by the people who show up — DJs, storytellers, journalists, musicians, and curious neighbors.
